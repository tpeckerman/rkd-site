About RKD

RKD is a retro arcade tucked at the bottom of the pedestrian mall in Zichron Yaakov. Step back in time with classic arcade games, claw machines, and good old-fashioned fun for kids and families.

Whether you're looking for an afternoon adventure or the perfect birthday party venue — we've got you covered.
